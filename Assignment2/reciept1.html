Rosemary & Honey Whiskey Cocktail

Recipe by Pavel Ciorici

Course:Drinks

/

Cuisine:American

/

Difficulty:Easy



Servings

4 servings



Prep time

10 minutes



Calories

11 kcal

Sed posuere consectetur est at lobortis. Donec ullamcorper nulla non metus auctor fringilla. Etiam porta sem malesuada magna mollis euismod.

Ingredients

For the Simple Syrup:

  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Honey
  • 2 Rosemary Sprigs

For the Cocktail:

  • 3 oz. Rye Whiskey
  • 1 oz. Rosemary and Honey Simple Syrup
  • 2 Dashes Aromatic Bitters
  • 1 Rosemary Sprig
  • Orange Peel, Garnish

Directions

  1. Add water, honey and sugar to a pot and bring to a boil. Once sugar dissolves, add rosemary sprigs and boil for an additional minute then lower to a simmer for 10 minutes.

  2. Add whiskey, bitters, and rosemary & honey simple syrup into a cocktail glass and stir.

  3. Add one large ice cube then garnish with an orange peel and rosemary sprig. Serve immediately.
